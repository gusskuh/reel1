**Products:**

**Login Kit:** Required prerequisite for Content Posting API. Enables OAuth 2.0 authentication to obtain access tokens for automated API requests.

**Content Posting API:** Core product for automated financial news reel posting. Uploads AI-generated video reels directly to TikTok as part of multi-platform content distribution.

**Scopes:**

**user.info.basic:** Retrieves basic account info during OAuth flow and verifies TikTok account is active before posting.

**user.info.stats:** Retrieves account statistics (followers, video count, engagement) to monitor account health and track performance.

**video.list:** Retrieves uploaded video list to verify successful uploads, check status, and manage content library with metadata.

**video.upload:** Primary scope for automated video posting. Uploads financial news reels (MP4, 1080x1920) with titles, descriptions, and hashtags via chunked upload.
